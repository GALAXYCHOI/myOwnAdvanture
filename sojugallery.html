Ways of drinking Soju

->Soju X Beer "SOJU BOMB"


which is a pint of beer with a shot glass full of soju dropped into it, mix that up, and drink whole thing one sip. Soju X Beer X Chiken "ChiSoMak"(Makju means beer in korean) is amazing.

->Soju X Rice wine X Sprite "SoMakSa" (Makguli means ricewine, sprite is called Sider in korea)


SoMakSa is for women's brink I think because that is soft, creamy, and sweet. SoMakSa is good matching with korean pancke. Some people call SoMakSa with pancake "couple" as always together. I love it.
